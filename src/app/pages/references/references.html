About Our Sources

All articles in The Digital Green Journal are carefully researched and written using credible, peer-reviewed sources and established environmental organizations. We believe in transparency and accountability, which is why we provide comprehensive references for all information presented.

Our sources include scientific research institutions, government agencies, NGOs, and academic publications dedicated to environmental protection and sustainability.

How We Use References

As an educational project, The Digital Green Journal follows best practices in citation and attribution:

  • All factual claims are backed by credible sources
  • References are provided in each article and on this page
  • We prioritize peer-reviewed research and official institutions
  • External links are regularly checked and updated for validity
  • We disclose any potential biases or limitations in our sources
